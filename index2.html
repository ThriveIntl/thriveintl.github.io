Connection the dots for an end to poverty.

Thrive brings together human stories and hard data to help cities and organizations succeed against poverty.

Charity is broken in America.

Every year, trillions of dollars are donated help people below the poverty line yet major challenges, such as homelessness, addiction, and access to living-wage jobs, remain present.

We don't know our neighbors.

Community Fragmentation

Organizations are unaware of whom they can partner with to achieve their goals. Even worse, they often struggle to know their community's needs and lack access to simple and clear data about their neighbors.

These groups are full of good people wanting to do good things, but no one organization can address all the complex challenges that are facing communities today; each must collaborate with others.

We're wasting our money and our talent.

Resource Allocation

We pour money into organizations that we believe in, hoping that our money will make a difference – yet poverty still runs rampant in our cities. Our cities who are full of talented people are being underutilized and marginalized as a result of community fragmentation.

Thrive exists to forge connections that empower individuals and organizations who are fighting poverty in their communities to their full potential.

How do we overcome these problems?

Connect people together for greater impact.

Learn about the solutions

We connect people together.

People and organizations that make up a city can’t work together without a knowledge of who is available to partner with and what each brings to the table.

We generate collaboration.

Organizational Partnering

Thrive connects organizations and individuals together towards mutual goals using data that’s collected from the local community. Each data point is embedded in human story, so we know the direct needs of the area.

Identify community resources

To fight poverty, cities need to know what organizations can do and where they can do it so that they can build coalitions. Thrive identifies and connects community partners based on what population segment they work with, what kind of work do they do, and their willingness to work with others.

Connecting neighbors together

By interviewing people in communities, Thrive gives them a voice by bringing their stories to life, all while capturing data on important metrics such as housing, healthcare, job availability and experiences within the community.

Learn about our tools

Our tools make complex data simple to understand.

Thrive has developed tools that deliver highly detailed community-based metrics that facilitate mutually beneficial cooperation between cities and organizations.

Mapping data for easy understanding.

We translate the information from helping organizations and a city’s population as told by the people to generate a comprehensive interactive visual representation of the collaborative potential of the city.

Soul

Simplified crisis information

Thrive uses data and story to understand areas in crisis. Our data collectors travel to areas hardest hit by natural disaster or industrial accident to gather real time data while acting as conduit of human connection to those suffering most. We put numbers in context, showing the human faces and stories they represent.

Learn more about Soul

Retain

Helping students succeed

Retain is a proprietary system that enables institutions to identify with a high degree of success which students will be at risk of dropping out of the school system/university, and how to positively address this challenge. This system is primarily based on the evaluation of specific indicators identified by our team, adjudicated through actuarial science, and tested in the real world.
