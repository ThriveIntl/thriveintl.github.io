We don't know our neighbors.

Community Fragmentation

Organizations lack access to simple and clear data about their neighbors. Communities and organizations have difficulty knowing how they can partner with each other to achieve their goals.

No single organization can address all the complex challenges that are facing communities today. Organizations must draw on the strengths of other organizations and the communities of which they are a part.
