Mapping data for easy understanding.

Thrive creates a venue for communities and organizations to hear one another effectively.We translate data from service organizations and the city’s residents to generate an interactive map of the city’s potential. This data highlights redundancy and potential synergy of services amongst organizations.

Soul

Simplified crisis information

Thrive gathers data and story to understand areas in crisis. Our data collectors travel to areas hit by natural disasters or industrial accidents to gather real-time data on behalf of intervention organizations. These data collectors act as a conduit of human connection, in both the immediate and long term. We put numbers in context, showing the human faces and stories they represent.

Retain

Helping students succeed

Retain is a proprietary system that enables institutions to identify with a high degree of success which students will be at risk of dropping out of the school system/university and how to positively address this challenge. This system is primarily based on the evaluation of specific indicators identified by our team, adjudicated through actuarial science, and tested in the real world.

Bringing community together

Thrive events

Thrive hosts fun, insightful, and creative events to help bring local communities together. By strengthening connections within the wider community, people become aware of mutual goals while introducing leaders to their counterparts from other parts of the community.
