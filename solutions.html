We catalyze collaboration.

Organizational Partnering

Thrive gathers stories and relevant data that clarifies the picture of the community being served. When organizations and individuals are made aware of both the relevant partners available and the assets, resources, and challenges of a community, all are empowered to collaborate more effectively.

Identify community resources

To fight poverty, cities and organizations need to know what they can do in order to build effective coalitions. Thrive identifies community partners based on what population segment they work with, what kind of work they do, and their willingness to work with others.

Connecting neighbors together

Thrive brings life to important metrics such as housing, healthcare and job availability through an interview process designed to capture the stories of the people living within the community.
